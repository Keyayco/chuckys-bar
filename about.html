The story.

Warm amber light. Wood, steel and concrete. Plates you can hear from across the room. Chuckys was built to feel like the best table at the best night, every night.

We opened Chuckys for the people who make a place. The regulars, the after-work crew, the Saturday sports die-hards, the crew who fight over the quiz answer every Thursday.

The kitchen leans into what we love: slow-cooked curries, honest South African plates and a bunny chow legend that keeps growing. The bar keeps things cold, quick and generous.

The room does the rest — Edison bulbs on wood, concrete floors, a stage that gets loud on weekends, and a pool table with a queue by 9pm.
