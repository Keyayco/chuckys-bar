Not just a bar.
A meeting place.

Chuckys is where the after-work crew, the Saturday sports die-hards, the quiz-night regulars and the live-music lovers all share the same table.

Warm amber light. Wood, steel and concrete. The kind of room where a plate of bunny chow lands with steam still rising and a cold draught is never more than a minute away.

We built Chuckys for the people who make a place — the loud table on a Friday, the quiet couple on a Tuesday, the crew who show up every Thursday to argue about the quiz.
